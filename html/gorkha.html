Gorkha - Bandipur- Palpa

Gorkha-Bandipur-Palpa Tour will provide the visitors with the golden opportunity to enjoy the celebrated view of the fine-looking surroundings of different places and mountain of Nepal. Gorkha was the old capital city of Nepal which will provide the visitors with the picturesque view of the ancient history and culture of Gorkhali people. The tour at the Gorkha and Bandipur region will be exciting if we get the chances to observe the ancient palaces of the Gorkha which was formerly used by Shah Dynasty Kings during their regime. The Gorkha is situated at the small mountains at the height of 1060m above sea level and also offers a fantastic view Gorkha palace, Gorkha bazaar, and Upallokot and Manakamana Temple. Bandipur is another highlight of the tour, where Prithivi Highway is the main trail to go there.
