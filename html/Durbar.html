Durbar Squares

Walkthrough the palace square and discover a treasure trove of art in stone, wood, and metal during your Patan, Bhaktapur and Kathmandu Durbar Square Tour. The Patan, Bhaktapur and Kathmandu Palace Museum, Golden Temple, Krishna Temple, etc., are some of the ancient gems of this medieval city. Moreover, visits to some of the oldest Buddhist shrines in the valley on the periphery of Patan Durbar square will enrich your experience.
