Pokhara - Ghandruk

Pokhara to Ghandruk is approx. 55 KM distance which can be reached within 4 hours. The road between Pokhara to Nayapul is blacktopped and the rest of the road is off-road drive. Road to Ghandruk goes through the edge of Modi Khola. Ghandruk is one of the popular Gurung villages which is situated in the height of 2012 meter from sea level. You can enjoy the magnificent view of Mt. Machhapuchre, Annapurna and Himchuli from here. Ghandruk Village is a popular destination for travelers these days in-terms of enjoyment.
