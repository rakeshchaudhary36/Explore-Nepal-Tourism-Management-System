Langtang Valley Trek

Langtang Valley Trek is one of the shortest treks in the Langtang region, which takes you on a beautiful journey to one of the isolated areas of Nepal. The Langtang Valley borders with Tibet in the north. Therefore, the region is strongly influenced by the lifestyle, culture, and tradition of its northern foil. It is home to Langtang National Park and a popular Gosainkunda pilgrimage site. During this trek, you can see the magnificent view of Langtang Lirung, Langtang Ri, Yala Peak, and many more.

☛Explore ancient monasteries such as Langtang Gompa and Kyanjin Gompa.
